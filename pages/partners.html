About CA Ganesh Prasad Nayak

Mr. Ganesh Prasad Nayak is a Fellow Member of The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India and has more than 26 years of professional experience in auditing, taxation, corporate law, project financing and financial advisory services.

He possesses rich expertise in statutory audits, forensic audits, internal audits, system audits, revenue analysis, restructuring and NCLT matters.

He has also worked on international project supervision assignments in countries like Ghana, Singapore, Thailand and Indonesia.

Over the years, he has represented clients before various tax and regulatory authorities and handled assignments for leading banks, corporations and institutions across India.

Professional Team

Our professional team consists of experienced auditors, taxation experts, accountants and compliance specialists dedicated to delivering accurate and timely services.

The team actively handles statutory audits, GST compliances, accounting systems, income tax matters, internal control reviews and financial reporting assignments for businesses across multiple industries.

Corporate Advisory Team

Our advisory division provides strategic consulting services in the areas of corporate law, FEMA, project financing, restructuring, business compliance and operational efficiency.

The team assists startups, SMEs and established enterprises with legal documentation, company incorporation, financial planning and regulatory advisory services tailored for sustainable business growth.
